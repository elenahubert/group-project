CAREERS & COST OF LIVING


Here, we have broken down the two most relevant topics for Northwestern upperclassmen looking at the future: their choice of career and the cost of living in major U.S. cities. This should serve as a guide to undecided students for selecting a career and a city that fits the financial outcome associated with that career.



Careers

This interactive illustrates the distribution of industries in which students find employment after graduation, displayed as a time series spanning from 2013 to 2023. Users can explore how industry trends have evolved over the decade.
